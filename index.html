About Me

I'm a full stack developer who leans more toward middle ware, backend, and database systems. I have a background in developing performant API endpoints. After gaining some experience in the industry, I have also decided to pursue a masters' degree in computer science through Georgia Tech.

I became interested in developing software when I was an analytics consultant at one of the largest e-commerce companies. One of my projects was working with backend engineers to generalize SQL queries for a drag and drop tool that was used to generate targeted marketing lists.

While I was working as a fraud prevention analyst, it became clear to me the importance of coding skills. Those skills allowed me to quickly make changes to a website, or database query to prevent further misuse of the site. During this role, I was also a student at Northwestern University's coding bootcamp.

While I have contributed to front end projects, I have also started contributing to open source projects in C#, Java, and Python. Not only is this valuable experience for me, it helps me stay up to date on latest trends in those coding languages and frameworks.
